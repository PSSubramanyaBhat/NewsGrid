The leader of the Uchiha, Fugaku Uchiha

Written By P.S Subramanya Bhat December 14, 2020
Entertainment

As leader of the Uchiha, Fugaku was focused on the clan's interest and welfare and had a strong sense of responsibility. His son, Sasuke, saw him as a strict man while he was seen as a father-figure to the rest of the clan. As Itachi was his successor as head of the clan, Fugaku spent much of his time focusing on the development of his eldest and little time with his youngest, but was very very proud of both of em.
